About Me

Hello. I'm a senior student studying user experience design at Simon Fraser University. As a digital product designer, I enjoy practising system-level interaction and visual design with a focus on product strategy. I had the privilege to learn and practice my craft of designing and building products at big technology companies such as Facebook, digital design agencies such as Input Logic and small startups like Flow. My passion is designing products that solve people’s problems and make their lives in today’s digital age better. From solving complex system-level problems down to sweating the details on creating intuitive, simple, aesthetically pleasing and most of all accessible user interfaces.
